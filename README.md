## ABOUT

**Quizzes 'n' Dragons** is a fast-paced quiz game that tests your knowledge on topics like Math, History, Biology, Pop Culture and others. You fight against one person in a battle of wits - 5 rounds, 5 different topics, 7 questions per round and 30 seconds to answer them. After each round you attack each other to lower your health, with your base damage being the amount of answers you got right. However, the way you attack is slightly different - you physically gesture a slash at each other using your phones! First to drain the other person's health bar wins - alternatively, if you have more health than the other person at the end of the 5 rounds, you win.

Magical dragons born with magnificent knowledge back you up in your journey for knowledge. The game allows you to select a Dragon to accompany you - this dragon will boost your damage output at the end of the round which held their favorite topic.

## How we built it
We used Android Studio, Kotlin and Jetpack Compose, and GitHub for version control. Alongside some help from AI tools, especially to help us understand and learn all the core principles and good programming and design practices a project like this is based on.
